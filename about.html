ABOUT TRAILER VALET

The Trailer Valet is a newly patented trailer tongue jack designed to help those that find it difficult to move their trailers in tight spaces. The Trailer Valet focuses on maneuverability, functionality, convenience, and safety. The Trailer Valet Multifunctional Tongue Jack serves a big community of people and unifies them. It serves the hunters, the fishers, the horseback riders, the campers, the RVers, the ATVers, the racers, the outdoor lovers of all ages, young and old.

Our goal is to provide our customers with great customer service and a products that they will love to use. We really try to focus on trying to make the lives of our customers easier so they can stop worrying about the hassles of moving their horse trailers, boat trailers, whatever trailers, and just enjoying what they love to do. Our mission is to provide our customers with quality trailer accessories that are innovative and built to last so people can spend less time worrying about their trailers and have more time on the road with their family.
